Overview

Raj Kumar Goel Institute of Technology (Pharmacy) started its journey under the able guidance of Late Shri. Raj Kumar Goel Ji, an eminent educationist, and a philanthropist, in 2004. His legacy is now carried forward by the current Chairman, Shri. Dinesh Kumar Goel Ji. RKGIT (Pharmacy) has 16 well equipped, multifocal Laboratories which are equipped with modern and sophisticated instruments like HPLC, UV spectrophotometer, Brookfield viscometer, Humidity cabinet, Lyophilizer, Rotary and single stroke tablet compression machine, Capsule filling and sealing machine, Laminar air flow cabinet, autoclave to name a few. The students are familiarized with these instruments, encountered in modern pharmaceutical industries. Herbal garden with more than 100 species of medicinal plants and CPCSEA approved Animal House is also available. A separate seminar hall with a seating capacity of 100 persons is available in institute’s building. The RKGIT (Pharmacy) digital library and stocks. Approximately 9,000 volumes with approx. 1700 titles. Subscribes 18 national and 6 International Journals in print version. The pharmacy student can freely access total 275 e-journals from DELNET and Science direct and 200 e-books along with latest multi-media learning resources. Book bank facilities are provided to all students. The students are provided with a platform to interact with eminent personalities from the industries by organising seminars, guest lectures and workshops on a regular basis. RKGIT (Pharmacy) promotes imparting quality education to aspiring pharmacists so that they can prove themselves to be worthy part of the rapidly evolving and dynamic Health Care System Globally.

Vision

To be recognized as a foremost Institution imparting quality Pharmacy education to aspiring pharmacists with right competencies, attitudes, skills, and knowledge, for the greater benefit of mankind

Key Highlights
  • To produce highly qualified and motivated graduates possessing fundamental knowledge and soft skills, who can provide sustainable solutions to the health care problems..
  • To develop partnerships with industries, eminent institutes, and government agencies in the field of pharmaceutical sciences.
  • To serve the community, at local, national, and international levels, with a deep awareness of our ethical responsibilities towards profession and society.
Why choose pharmacy as a career?

A Graduate degree in Pharmacy offers a vast number of rewarding career opportunities in the Health-care sector. Indian Pharmaceutical Industry is a rapidly growing sector, second only to IT, with a 15% annual growth rate and exports crossing Rs.15,000 crores. Annual demand of pharmacist is increasing in leaps and bounds. Pharmacists are needed by the Pharmaceutical Industry in different areas such as Manufacturing, Quality Assurance and Control, Regulatory Affairs, R&D and Marketing and Sales. Pharmacists are also needed in Government Regulatory Bodies concerned with Drug Control. In the Health Care Sector, a Pharmacy Graduate or Postgraduate can find employment in the Hospitals, in Retail, in Clinical Research and in Pharmacovigilance. Top pharmaceutical companies hiring pharmacist include Ranbaxy, Cadila, MicroLab, Johnson & Johnson, Pfizer, GlaxoSmithKline, Roche, Sanofi-Aventis, Novartis, AstraZeneca, Abbott Laboratories, Merck, USV Pharma, etc.
